# What's New in This Version (iOS/Android)

We are thrilled to bring the MapCombiner experience to mobile! Our first major release is packed with features to make your road trips and site visits smoother than ever:

- **Bypass the 10-Stop Limit**: Finally, plan massive journeys with more than 10 stops. Combine multiple route batches into one single, continuous navigation session.
- **Native App Optimization**: A high-performance interface designed specifically for mobile, featuring a sleek, modern dark-theme with glassmorphism effects.
- **One-Tap 'Open in Maps'**: Seamlessly transition from our app directly into the official Google Maps app to start your journey.
- **Intelligent Route History**: Never lose a complex plan again. Access your recently combined routes right from the dashboard.
- **Enhanced Native Sharing**: Share your combined itineraries with travel partners or clients using the native system share dialog for faster collaboration.
- **Support for MapParser**: Easily parse and analyze your route data via built-in integration with Travel Tracker's MapParser.
- **Privacy & Speed**: Lightning-fast local processing with zero server-side tracking. Your route data stays on your device.
- **Fix Bugs**: Fix some bugs.

For more details and updates, visit: https://mapcombiner.travel-tracker.org/
