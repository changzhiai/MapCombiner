# App Store Connect Description

Planning a massive road trip or managing a busy delivery route? Frustrated by the strict 10-destination limit in Google Maps? MapCombiner is the ultimate travel utility that instantly merges multiple Google Maps route URLs into one continuous, seamless journey!

**BREAK THE 10-STOP BARRIER**
Google Maps is the gold standard for navigation, but its limitation to just 10 stops can be a major roadblock for power users. Whether you’re a traveler exploring the country, a delivery driver organizing daily drops, or a professional coordinator, MapCombiner is the missing bridge you’ve been looking for. Forget the hassle of switching between multiple navigation sessions or trying to remember where your last route ended. MapCombiner stitches your plans together, launching a unified, limitless route straight into your native Google Maps app without losing any waypoints.

**KEY FEATURES:**
*   **Limitless Combinations**: Paste as many Google Maps links as you need. Our intelligent algorithm extracts every waypoint and stitches them into one master itinerary.
*   **One-Tap Navigation**: Once combined, a single tap launches your entire journey directly in the official Google Maps app.
*   **Native Experience**: No need to learn a new map system. We use the Google Maps you already know and love for the actual navigation.
*   **Privacy-First Design**: Your routes are your business. MapCombiner processes all data locally on your device. We do not store your locations or routes on any servers.
*   **No Friction**: No accounts, no sign-ups, and no messy logins. Open the app and start combining immediately.
*   **Clean & Modern Interface**: A premium, ad-free experience designed for speed and ease of use while you're on the move.
*   **Smart History**: Stay organized with a built-in history of your recently combined routes, making it easy to restart a leg of your journey if you get interrupted.

**WHO IS MAPCOMBINER FOR?**
*   **The Ultimate Road Tripper**: Planning a cross-country adventure with 30+ scenic overlooks? Batch your plans and combine them for a seamless drive.
*   **Delivery & Service Pros**: Couriers, florists, and estate agents can organize 20+ daily stops in seconds, reducing downtime between destinations.
*   **Travel Influencers & Professionals**: Create and share massive, multi-stop itineraries with followers or clients using a single link.
*   **Field Researchers**: Perfect for site inspections or researchers visiting dozens of locations in a single day.

**HOW IT WORKS – AS EASY AS 1-2-3:**
1.  **Plan** your routes in Google Maps (as many batches of 10 stops as you need).
2.  **Paste** the URLs for each route into MapCombiner.
3.  **Combine & Go!** Hit the combine button to generate your master route and launch it directly into Google Maps with a single tap.
